Spaghetti Carbonara

Spaghetti alla Carbonara

This is the real spaghetti carbonara - with eggs and bacon. Coal men from Umbria "Carbonari," selling charcoal to the Romans, brought this dish to Lazio. The Romans adopted it and naturally carbonara became famous worldwide!

This recipe is by Antonio Carluccio antonio-carluccio.com

Ingredients

  • 220g Spaghetti or Spaghettoni (the largest spaghetti)
  • 25g Guanciale (the cheek of the pork) or pancetta (Italian bacon) cut into small cubes
  • 2tbsp olive oil
  • 2 eggs
  • 50g Parmesan cheese (or aged Pecorino) freshly grated
  • Freshly ground black pepper

Steps

  1. Cook the pasta in a large pan of boiling salted water until al dente.
  2. Meanwhile, heat the oil in a pan and fry the guanciale or pancetta until crisp.
  3. Lightly beat the eggs in a large bowl with the grated cheese and pepper.
  4. When the pasta is ready, drain and add to the pan with the guanciale, mix well to coat everything.
  5. Take of the heat and allow to cool slightly.
  6. Then add the egg and cheese mixture, stir to coat the pasta and serve immediately.
  7. Enjoy!
