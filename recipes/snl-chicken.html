SNL Chicken

Rosemary Thyme Chicken & Garlic Mash Potatoes

If your stuck at home and you have a little time to kill then this quick and easy recipe is for you.

Please note: when cooking in the kitchen don't use your computer or leave your kids unattended!

Ingredients

  • The internet (optional, in case of a fire)
  • 1 whole Chicken
  • 2 sprigs of fresh rosemary
  • 200g sautéed onions
  • 300g Dutch cream potatoes
  • 30g butter
  • A small bunch of chives
  • Imagination (in case you don't finish cooking)

Steps

  1. Put the rosemary and sautéed onions onto the chicken.
  2. Place the chicken into a preheated oven at 400°C for about 30 mins.
  3. Optional step: Now you'll have a little time to kill so if you need to search the internet at this time, go ahead.
  4. Start boiling the potatoes in generously salted water for about 4 mins.
  5. Double check your oven is actually set to 400°c and not 4000°C. If it's the later feel free to call your local fire department from your favorite website?
  6. Mix your fresh chives with the butter.
  7. Now is the time to use your Imagination because this is definitely not the correct way to roast a chicken!
